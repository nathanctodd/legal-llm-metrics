Metric Summary
--------------
Metric: Calibration (ECE)
Result: 0.3484

Why it Matters:
Calibration measures whether the model's stated confidence matches its
actual accuracy. High overconfidence is a safety risk in legal AI
because incorrect but highly confident responses may mislead users.

Method:
LLM-as-judge (truthful Y/N) with ECE computed over 64 TruthfulQA-Law questions.

Limitations:
- Small sample size (n = 64)
- Self-reported confidence values
- Possible LLM-as-judge evaluation error
- Adversarial questions increase calibration difficulty
